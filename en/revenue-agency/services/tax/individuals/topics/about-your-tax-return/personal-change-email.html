Update your information with the CRA

Change your email address

 

The email address you provide is the one we will use to tell you that important changes were made on your account or that you have new mail to view in My Account. My Account users are required to have an email address on file with the CRA to help protect their accounts from fraudulent activity.

You can only have one email address on file at a time.

Online

Change your email address online using My Account

Sign in to My Account Register
  • Select Notification preferences
  • Select Contact information
  • Select Edit

If you can't sign in to your account

If your phone number has changed and you can't access your account, you will have to update your information by phone.

For more help with your account, go to: Help guides for CRA sign-in accounts

Make a change for someone else

To change an email address online for someone else, including a spouse or family member, you must be an authorized representative and use Represent a Client.

By phone

Before you call

Have your social insurance number (SIN) ready to verify your identity. Also, sign into your CRA account or gather the following information:

  • Full name and date of birth
  • Complete address
  • Assessed tax return, notice of assessment or reassessment, other tax document

Make a change for someone else

If you’re calling on behalf of someone else, they must be present on the call to give their consent, or you must be authorized to call on their behalf.

For details: Authorize a representative

Update your other personal information

You may also need to change your home or mailing address, phone number, or other details with the CRA. To update more of your personal information, go to: Update your information with the CRA

Change your email address with other government departments

The CRA will not share your email address or other personal information, including direct deposit, with other government departments.

You need to contact each government department or agency to give them your new information. Go to: Change an address with a different department or agency
