Types of accounts

There are 3 different types of accounts you can register for to gain access to CRA services.

They are:

My Account
View your personal income tax and benefit information and manage your tax affairs online.
Read more: About My Account
My Business Account
Interact electronically with the CRA on various business accounts.
Read more: About My Business Account
Represent a Client
Access to both individual and business tax information for another entity.
Read more: About Represent a Client

Hours of service

The CRA online services are available 21 hours a day.

Hours of operation
  • Newfoundland Standard Time: 7:30 am to 4:30 am
  • Atlantic Standard Time: 7 am to 4 am
  • Eastern Standard Time: 6 am to 3 am
  • Central Standard Time: 5 am to 2 am
  • Mountain Standard Time: 4 am to 1 am
  • Pacific Standard Time: 3 am to 12 am (midnight)

Due to daily maintenance activities, the online services are not available for three hours a day from 3 am to 6 am (Eastern time).
